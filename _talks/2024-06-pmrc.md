Presented research on how different forms of local government (e.g., mayor-council, council-manager) affect health outcomes and health disparities through their impact on governmental responsiveness. This work contributes to understanding the political determinants of health by examining institutional structures.

Key Topics
======
* Forms of government and health outcomes
* Governmental responsiveness
* Political determinants of health
* Local government institutions
* Health disparities
