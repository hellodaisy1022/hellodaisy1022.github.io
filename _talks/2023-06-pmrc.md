Presented research examining whether social capital has differential effects across racial groups in the context of COVID-19 mortality. This work questions the assumption that social capital uniformly benefits all community members and explores potential racialized dimensions of social capital.

Key Topics
======
* Social capital and COVID-19
* Racial health disparities
* COVID-19 mortality
* Community-level factors
* Health equity
