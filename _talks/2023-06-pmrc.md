This research aims to understand whether community-level social capital is associated with reduced COVID-19 mortality across racial groups, whether citizen political ideology conditions this relationship, and how the effects of overall social capital differ from Black social capital in shaping Black COVID-19 mortality and influenza vaccination outcomes.
