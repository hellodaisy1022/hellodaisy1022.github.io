Instructor of Record for Public Administration in American Society, an introductory undergraduate course covering the fundamentals of public administration, the role of government in society, and contemporary issues in public sector management.

Course Topics
======
* Foundations of public administration
* The role of government in American society
* Public policy making and implementation
* Organizational structure and management
* Public service ethics and values
* Contemporary challenges in public administration
