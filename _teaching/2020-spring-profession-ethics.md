**Role:** Teaching Assistant

Assisted with two online graduate courses in Spring 2020:

* **The Profession of Public Administration:** Introduction to the field of public administration, covering professional standards, career paths, and the role of public administrators in society.

* **Public Service Ethics:** Examination of ethical issues and dilemmas facing public administrators, including professional responsibility, accountability, and ethical decision-making frameworks.

**Semester:** Spring 2020
