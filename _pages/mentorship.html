Mentoring Philosophy

  • Passion: I first try to see what drives each mentee and guide them toward their ikigai—the intersection of what they love, what they are good at, what the world needs, and what they can be paid for.
  • Ability: I train mentees to solve problems independently, so they experience the tension and joy of discovery, which motivates them to chase bigger questions and harder problems.
  • Character: I encourage mentees to stay resilient and positive, reminding them that challenges are part of the journey and that it is important to keep seeing the beauty and meaning in the world.
